What is Flow?

Flow is Accounting Simplified

When our team began creating Flow we had two main goals: Include as many features as possible, and keep it simple! Finances can be overwhelming for a lot of people, but Flow is designed to eliminate the complexity of accounting and bookkeeping.

The Flow app is very easy to learn and anyone can feel like an accounting expert after just a couple uses!
